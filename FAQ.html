Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rent and leave the State of Tennessee? +

No, our vehicles are only permitted in the State of Tennessee.

When will I get my security deposit back? +

About 7-10 days after returning the rented vehicle.

How much is the security deposit? +

The security deposit is $250.

Which payment methods are available? +

We accept all credit card types with payment in USD.

What are the minimum age requirements for renting? +

You must be at least 18 years of age to rent, but for those under the age of 25 there is $120 Under Age Fee.

Is smoking permitted? +

No! Smoking is not permitted in the rented vehicle.
